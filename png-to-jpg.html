FAQ

Will I lose quality?

JPG uses lossy compression, but at our 92% quality setting the difference is virtually invisible for photos. For pixel-perfect graphics or text, consider keeping PNG.

What happens to transparency?

JPG doesn't support transparency. Transparent areas become white. If you need transparency, export as WebP instead.

How much smaller will the file be?

Typically 5-10x smaller. A 5MB PNG screenshot might become 500KB as JPG — perfect for email or uploading.
